The Ministry

This institution was founded in 2010 by the Servant of God, Rev. Dr. Henry Poto.
While he was still an unbeliever wallowing in sin, he was invited by his friend to a Full-Gospel party and right there in that event he gave his life to Christ and answered the call from God to establish an Outreach Ministry that would spread the Love of God round the World.

    "Our mission is to reach-out to unsaved souls and change their lives and Destinies with the Gospel"
